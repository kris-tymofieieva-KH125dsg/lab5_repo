## My favorite math formulas
**Pythagoras' formula**

$$
c^2=a^2+b^2
$$

**Difference of squares formula**

$$
a^2-b^2=(a-b)(a+b)
$$

**Formula for the square sum**

$$
(a+b)^2=a^2+2ab+b^2      
$$

**Formula for the square difference**

$$
(a-b)^2=a^2-2ab+b^2  
$$

**Heron's formula**

$$
S=\sqrt{p(p-a)(p-b)(p-c)}; p=\frac{a+b+c}{2}
$$
